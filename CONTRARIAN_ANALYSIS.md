### 1. ACTUAL KDP SALES DATA (Agent: web-research-specialist)

- $110,000 total... over **12+ years** = $9,000/year average
- That's the OUTLIER, not the norm

**Reality for most publishers:**
- **95% of KDP authors** do NOT reach substantial financial success
- Median KDP book sells **fewer than 250 copies LIFETIME**
- Majority earn **less than $300/month** even after years
- One publisher reported: **"$0.43 every couple months"** from PD editions

**Public Domain Specifics:**
- Amazon **actively suppresses** undifferentiated PD content
- 35% royalty ONLY (no 70% option)
- Cannot use Kindle Unlimited
- At $0.99 pricing: **$0.35 per sale** after Amazon's cut

## What Would Actually Work

If you're committed to this space, here's what the research suggests:

### Strategy Shifts Required

1. **Target obscure works, not famous ones**
   - Avoid The Maltese Falcon (will have 100+ competing editions)
   - Find 1930 works with search volume but <50 existing editions
   - Niche > famous

2. **Build audience BEFORE publishing**
   - 6-12 months of content marketing
   - Email list of 1,000+ before launch
   - Social media presence in literary communities

3. **Budget for advertising**
   - $300-500/month minimum
   - Without ads, you're invisible in Amazon search

4. **Go wide from Day 1**
   - Don't rely solely on Amazon
   - Publish simultaneously on D2D, IngramSpark
   - Build direct sales capability

5. **Focus on premium physical**
   - Higher margins
   - Less price competition
   - Collectors/gift market
   - Justify $25-50 price point

6. **Create genuine value**
   - Not just "annotations" - scholarly quality
   - Commission original illustrations
   - Partner with academics
   - Make something that actually deserves to exist
